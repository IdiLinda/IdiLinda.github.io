About

PlanAble is an app that uses it's unique QR code camera scanner to create and organize a schedule for college students. Our app will eliminate all the chances of errors because the app’s QR code reader, synchronizes the dates on the course syllabus with the dates on your personal calendar. Afterwards the student gets pre-set notifications to help them keep track of what’s due when.



With the collaboration of our partner colleges, professors will put QR codes on every syllabus they hand out to students that our app will scan, plan, and be able to make the lives of millions of college students easier!
